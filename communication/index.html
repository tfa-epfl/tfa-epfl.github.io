Communication & Podcast Dvision

The Quant Research Track is at the heart of TFA. It operates on the basis of a set of clearly defined methodological principles designed to produce credible and reproducible results. The Quant Research Track is managed by a team of experienced researchers who are responsible for designing and implementing research projects.

What the Division Does

The division acts as TFA's media and editorial branch. It produces video interviews, podcast episodes, educational market content, event coverage, and long-form discussions with professionals from the financial industry.

Video Production

Production of interviews, finance explainers, conference recordings, and market-focused educational content.

Podcast Series

Long-form discussions with traders, researchers, founders, portfolio managers, and quantitative professionals.

Institutional Communication

Communication around events, publications, partnerships, recruiting opportunities, and TFA initiatives.
